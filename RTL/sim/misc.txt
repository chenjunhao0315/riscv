QuickStart:
Presim : python3.6 run_tp.py --case simple
Gatesim: python3.6 run_tp.py --case simple --gate True

Available case:
Presim: simple xtest03 xtest04 xtest07 xtest09 memory_test aes256
Gatesim: simple xtest03 (The rest of test will generate big .vcd)

Usage:
python3.6 run_tp.py --case [case_name in SW/test/examples/] --gate [True/False to run the gate sim]
